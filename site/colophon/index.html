Accessibility and sustainability

  • Compliant with the Web Content Accessibility AAA Guidelines, the current WAVE accessibility rank: #7,280 of 1,000,000.
  • The homepage loads in less than a second and weighs 92 KB. The entire source is 13.8 MB excluding videos.
  • 0.01 g of CO2 is produced when someone visits the homepage.
  • Featured on Lowwwcarbon.
  • Hosting running on green energy.

Privacy

I don't collect personal data, there is no analytics software, no ads, no affiliate links, no tracking scripts, no pop-ups, no modal windows. A content security policy is in place.
