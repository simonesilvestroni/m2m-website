‘13 Days’ music video

The Cuban crisis of 1962. President Kennedy announces proof of a build-up of Soviet missiles on the island. The world is one step from nuclear war.

  31 May 2020

The threat of a pre-emptive strike to America is real, as the level of military readiness is set to DEFCON 2. 13 Days is a single from the concept album After 1989: A Trip To Freedom, released 9 November 2019.
