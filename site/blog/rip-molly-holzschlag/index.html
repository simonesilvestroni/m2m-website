RIP Molly Holzschlag

Very sad to learn that Molly Holzschlag died yesterday. She was one of the most influential advocates of web standards and accessibility.

  6 Sep 2023

Everyone has something to contribute to the World Wide Web. Why? Because the Web is of us. Whatever we are as humans is now manifest in the Web: our beauty, hatred, fragility, and ferocity; our kindness, cruelty, confusion, and clarity. Our wars. Our peace. Molly Holzschlag
