Bass: ‘In Our Dreams’ by Antiquity

‘In Our Dreams’ is the new single by Gerald Duchene. Vocals by Dan Ecclestone, bass by Minutes to Midnight.

  30 Jul 2020

I always enjoy being a part of Gerald’s music. This song was particularly exciting to work on, since I rarely have the chance to play bass where drums are nowhere to be heard. I believe the result is stunning.
