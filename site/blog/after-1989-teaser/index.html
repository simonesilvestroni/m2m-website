‘After 1989’ teaser video

30 years after the opening of the Berlin Wall, ‘After 1989’ is a real-life story about imprisonment and liberty. Watch the video teaser.

  8 Nov 2019

Video teaser

If you prefer, watch the video on YouTube

Cambridge, 9 November 2019. Minutes to Midnight's concept album After 1989: A Trip To Freedom is out on the 30th anniversary of the opening of the Berlin Wall. It's the story of how a young man escaped Germany in 1945, while his grandson made the other way round, looking for answers that he could only find in 2017.
