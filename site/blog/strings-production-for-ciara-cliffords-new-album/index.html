Strings production: Ciara Clifford

I recorded and co-produced strings arrangements for Clifford’s eponymous debut album, in collaboration with Paolo Clementi.

  11 Jan 2020

Production notes

Building on the same workflow adopted for Mally Harpaz's solo album Zoom In Zoom Out, we added an electric viola to the mix, and switched from the Miroslav Philarmonik library to my current Hollywood Strings Orchestra strings library.

The record was then mixed by Shuta Shinoda at the Strongroom, in London.
