‘Love Field’ reviewed on YHHTMY podcast

YHHTMY podcast selected my song ‘Love Field’ from the album ‘After 1989’ for their latest episode.

  27 Jan 2020

About YHHTMY

You Haven't Heard This Music Yet is a podcast exploring unheard music, which they feel is as good if not better than that of the mainstream. With challenges laughs and banter along the way. A quote from their review of Love Field:

It really impacted me, I listened to the entire album which is something I rarely do with submissions. YHHTMY
