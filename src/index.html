About

I'm Simone, a gen X with a straight to the point attitude. Based in the UK, I play bass and design sound under the moniker Minutes to Midnight. It's a reference to the Doomsday Clock, a connection with the world I grew up in during the Cold War.

In previous lives I worked as a game audio designer and a web developer. Today my day job is with the University of Cambridge.

To dig deeper, I wrote a personal manifesto.
